Mash

Description

This is the ultimute, smoothest mash potatoes you will ever eat.

This recipe yields roughly 5 servings, if you feel like sharing.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cream or Milk
  • Seasoning
  • Of course Potatoes

Directions

  1. Peel the potatoes.
  2. Cut into quaters and place into cold salted water in a large pot.
  3. Boild the potatoes until tender.
  4. Mash the potatoes while gradually adding the other ingredients.
